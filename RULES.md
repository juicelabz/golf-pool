# The 2026 YTOWN Pool Invitational Rules** 

## Team Selection

Fill out the entry form at [https://forms.gle/1TW2TBkiv2jfWzau7](https://forms.gle/1TW2TBkiv2jfWzau7)

**PLEASE NOTE**: **The players you select will be your team for the duration of the pool. Once the pool season begins no changes can be made to your team. If a player leaves for another tour, there will be no substitutions. Only performance in the official events found in The 2026 Golf Pool Invitational Schedule of Events is counted.**

- Select only **one** (1) player from each category, 1 through 7, by clicking/checking the box next to the player you wish to choose on the entry form.   
- Categories 8, 9 & 10 are Open Picks, or “write-in” picks. Select any **three** (3) individual players that are **not** already available in categories 1 through 7 as your Open Picks. **Players available in categories 1 through 7 are NOT eligible as Open Picks**. **Scottie Scheffler is NOT eligible for the pool this year in any category or as a write-in.**   
- Completed entry forms are due by 12:00PM ET Thursday, January 15, 2026** to the google link  
- The $100.00 entry fee needs to be sent to the Commissioner via Venmo/Square/Paypal PRIOR to the completion of the first event of the season, January 18, 2026.

## [Schedule of Events](SCHEDULE_OF_EVENTS.md)

The 2026 YTOWN Pool Invitational is comprised of 34 tournaments.   

- The first event that counts for 2026 is the Sony Open, beginning Thursday, January 15, 2026. The final event that counts for 2026 is The Tour Championship in Atlanta, beginning Thursday, August 27, 2026.
- The 33 tournaments are broken down into 5 individual Scoring Segments. Please refer to the Schedule of Events file for full details.
